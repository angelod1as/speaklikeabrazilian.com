--- title: Mother's week expressions category: blog author: SLBR team --- Last week we had Mother's week here at SLBR. Here's the list of expressions we've received. **[Dar de mamar](http://www.speaklikeabrazilian.com/expression/define?e=Dar%20de%20mamar)** Same as "amamentar". It means breastfeed. - Você pode ir lá abrir a porta enquanto eu dou de mamar pro Nuno? **[Chá de bebê](http://www.speaklikeabrazilian.com/expression/define?e=Ch%C3%A1%20de%20beb%C3%AA)** A baby shower. - A Maria está grávida! - Oba, vamos chamar as meninas e organizar um chá de bebê. **[Mãe coruja](http://www.speaklikeabrazilian.com/expression/define?e=M%C3%A3e%20coruja)** A doting mother. - Sua mãe é maior mãe coruja hein? Veio até o Nepal pra recepcionar você no fim da sua escalada. **[Nem um piu](http://www.speaklikeabrazilian.com/expression/define?e=Nem%20um%20piu)** Not a sound. What sometimes your mother tells you when she's on the phone and doesn't want to listen another sound. - Ssshhh menino. Nem um piu agora que vou ligar pra sua tia.
